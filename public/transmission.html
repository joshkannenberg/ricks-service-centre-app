Transmission Repair & Service

Just like your engine, your transmission requires regular maintenance to keep it running properly. Our service includes a visual inspection of the transmission pan & lines, a fluid level/condition check, and a road test to ensure it is working properly.

Transmission Repair or Replacement

Our team of experienced technicians can diagnose and repair any transmission problem. We can also replace or rebuild any transmission. When rebuilding a transmission, it is completely dissassembled and all parts are inspected for wear and cleaned, or replaced depending on their condition. When replacing a transmission, we offer factory replacement options and used units.

Transmission Service

It is important to have your transmission serviced at the intervals specified by the vehicle manufacturer. This service generally includes a transmission fluid and filter change and road test but can also include a scan of the system and relearn if necessary.
