Transmission Service & Repair

If you are experiencing transmission problems, let our team of experienced professionals diagnose and correct these issues. We can repair and service both foreign and domestic vehicles.

Auto Services

Check Engine Light

A check engine light means you should have your vehicle looked at immediately. Rick's Service Centre has the tools and expertise required to find and diagnose check engine light codes.

Brakes

From brake pads and rotors to ABS systems, our technicians can perform brake repairs on any kind of vehicle.

Transmission Service & Repair

Just like your engine, your transmission requires regular maintenance to keep it running properly. Our service includes a visual inspection of the transmission pan & lines, a fluid level/condition check, and a road test to ensure it is working properly.

Oil Change

By replacing your engine oil and filter based on the manufacturers guidelines, you can extend the life of your engine. We replace the engine oil, filters, check tire pressure, and top up all other fluids.

New Tires & Tire Repairs

We offer tires from leading brands, from original equipment tires to the finest replacement tires available in the aftermarket.

AC & Heating Repairs

If you are having issues with your AC or heating in your vehicle, we can fix it. We can service both R134a and R1234yf A/C systems.

Alignments

Wheel alignments by experienced technicians using the most advanced technology. Call now to get a quote or schedule an appointment.

Steering & Suspension

Have your steering and suspension system inspected to ensure the safety of your vehicle. Maintaining your steering and suspension system will extend the life of your tires and keep you safe on the road.

Batteries, Starters, & Charging

We can test your car battery to make sure it is holding a charge and replace it if necessary. The starter and alternator can also have issues.
